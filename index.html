The shop

C. Monte 412 · Campo 4

Built for the work.

The shop was purpose-built for one thing: doing automotive work properly. Tall doors, bright bays and a floor clean enough that the NB logo is painted right on it.

  • Modern lifts in every bay — two-post lifts plus mobile column lifts for heavy and lifted trucks.
  • Real equipment — on-site brake lathe, tire and alignment gear, engine cranes and supports.
  • Room to do it right — space for engine-out jobs and longer builds without rushing the reassembly.
