About us

Campo 4 · Chihuahua

Built on doing it right.

NB Autoworks is a purpose-built repair shop on the Campo 4 road — created so the campos and the Cuauhtémoc region don't have to drive to the city for dealer-level service.

Our mission

Fix it properly. Charge it honestly. Hand it back better than it came in.

The story

From the ground up.

Around here, everything runs on machines — and machines only stay honest if somebody looks after them properly. NB Autoworks was started by people who grew up doing exactly that, and who wanted a shop worthy of the work: bright, clean, and equipped like the dealers in the city.

So we poured the floor, painted the logo on it, striped the walls in NB green and filled the bays with proper equipment — modern lifts, mobile columns for the heavy trucks, a brake lathe, engine cranes and factory-level diagnostics.

Today the shop looks after everything from farm trucks and daily drivers to European SUVs and serious performance builds. The vehicles change; the standard doesn't.
