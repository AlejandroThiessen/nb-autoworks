Services

Scheduled service → heavy repair

Everything under one roof.

One shop for the whole vehicle — book the small jobs online, and talk to us about the big ones. If it isn't listed here, ask anyway; chances are we've done it.

Maintenance & oil service

The cheapest repair is the one you never need. We keep daily drivers, work trucks and fleet vehicles on schedule — and every service includes a look around the whole vehicle, so surprises get caught while they're still small.

  • Oil & filter service — conventional, synthetic & diesel-spec oils
  • Coolant, brake, transmission & differential fluid service
  • Belts, hoses, filters, wipers & bulbs
  • Multi-point inspection with every visit — photos on request
  • Most maintenance visits are same-day in and out

Diagnostics & electrical

A check-engine light is a symptom, not a diagnosis. We scan, test and verify before recommending anything — you get a straight answer about what's wrong, what it costs, and what can safely wait.

  • Check-engine lights, misfires & drivability complaints
  • No-start, no-crank & intermittent electrical faults
  • Sensors, wiring, modules & connectors
  • Batteries, starters, alternators & parasitic draws
  • Clear written findings before any repair begins

Brakes

Brakes are the one system where “good enough” isn't. We machine rotors and drums in-house on our Ranger brake lathe — so a brake job that other shops send away for gets done here, the same day.

  • Pads, rotors, drums & calipers — all makes
  • On-site rotor & drum machining (Ranger RL-8500)
  • Brake lines, hoses, master cylinders & fluid flushes
  • ABS & brake-warning-light diagnosis
  • Heavy-duty & towing brake upgrades

Suspension, steering & tires

Whether it's a worn ball joint on a farm truck or a full lift kit with wheels and rubber to match, the goal is the same: a vehicle that tracks straight, rides right and wears its tires evenly.

  • Shocks, struts, springs & steering components
  • Lift & leveling kits for trucks and SUVs
  • Wheel & tire mounting, balancing & TPMS service
  • Wheel bearings, CV axles & driveline vibration
  • Alignment checks with every suspension job

Engine & transmission

The big jobs are where the shop earns its name. Engine swaps, rebuilds, transmissions and head gaskets — pulled, documented, and reassembled to spec, with room in the bay to do it without rushing.

  • Engine replacement, swaps & rebuilds
  • Transmission service, repair & replacement
  • Head gaskets, timing chains & oil leaks
  • Cooling systems, radiators & water pumps
  • Transfer cases, differentials & driveline

Performance & upgrades

From bolt-ons to twin-turbo builds, performance work here gets the same discipline as repair work: planned supporting mods, quality hardware, and results that hold together on real roads.

  • Turbo & supercharger installs and upgrades
  • Exhaust, intake, intercooler & cooling upgrades
  • Aero & carbon-fiber installs
  • Drag setups — wheels, tires & traction hardware
  • Supporting mods planned before the power goes in
